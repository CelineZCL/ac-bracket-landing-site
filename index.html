Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What protection do you offer if a product failure occurs? +
A: We stand behind our engineering with a $1,000,000 Product Liability Insurance policy. This coverage provides ultimate peace of mind for importers and contractors, ensuring that your business is protected against unforeseen structural risks.
Q: How do you ensure structural integrity against metal fatigue caused by constant AC unit vibration? +
A: With 29 years of stamping expertise, we address vibration through three engineering layers:

Stress-Distribution Geometry: Our brackets feature reinforced ribs at critical load points to disperse cyclic stress and prevent micro-cracking.

High-Tensile Steel: We use premium coils with controlled tolerances in our 25,000 m² facility to ensure a perfect fit that minimizes frictional wear.

Anti-Vibration Hardware: Every kit includes high-damping rubber pads and Dacromet-coated anti-loosening bolts that maintain constant tension even under extreme temperature fluctuations.
Q: Are your brackets suitable for coastal or high-humidity environments? +
A: Yes. We specialize in superior corrosion resistance. While standard brackets fail early, our systems pass a 240-hour Neutral Salt Spray (NSS) Test. We offer 304/316 Stainless Steel and Hot-Dip Galvanized options to ensure a rust-free lifespan of 10-15+ years in harsh environments.
Q: Are you a trading company or a direct manufacturer? +
A: We are a direct manufacturer with nearly three decades of experience. We control the entire supply chain—from raw steel coils and precision stamping to automated surface treatment and final QC—ensuring factory-direct pricing and consistent quality.
Q: Do you support OEM/ODM and custom branding? +
A: Absolutely. We can customize sizes, colors (powder coating), and stamp your Logo directly onto the brackets. We also provide customized professional packaging for distributors to enhance shelf appeal.
Q: What is your typical lead time and MOQ? +
A: Our standard MOQ is 500 pcs per model. For first-time trial orders, we offer flexible MOQ — contact us to discuss your specific needs. Thanks to our large-scale production capacity, typical lead times range from 20 to 30 days.
